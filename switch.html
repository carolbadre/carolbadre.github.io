How it works

You'll complete three timed rounds. In each round, click the buttons in the correct order as fast as you can.

  • Round 1 — click numbers 1 to 10 in order
  • Round 2 — click letters A to J in order
  • Round 3 — alternate between the two: 1, A, 2, B...

At the end you'll see how much the task-switching in round 3 slowed you down compared to rounds 1 and 2 combined.
