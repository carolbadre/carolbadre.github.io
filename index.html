I am a first year PhD student at The CUNY Graduate Center. I am interested in geometric group theory and low-dimensional topology.
I also have some background in knot theory, CAT(0)-cube complexes and trisections.
In the past I have been interested in groups acting on non-positively curved and tree like spaces and the resulting structure and decomposition of these groups.
I have previously studied mathematics at The University of Melbourne and The University of Sydney.
I enjoy drawing and visualising mathematics, primarily by hand and also by programming.
